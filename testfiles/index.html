Las Cruces, New Mexico

Las Cruces is located in the south of New Mexico, fairly close to both the Texas and Mexico border. It is also know as The City of the Crosses . Las Cruces is a generally quiet city with a population of around 100,000 people. Although the city is calm, it's weather during all seasons can be fairly unpredictable. Las Cruces is an agriculture-oriented city as it has many large farms of various types and it's university has extensive agricultural programs.

I have been living in Las Cruces for most of my life since I moved here from Albuquerque. While there aren't an abudance of attractions in Las Cruces, there are a few places I can recommend to vistors. The Farmers & Crafts Market on Main Street is a great place to go on the weekends if you enjoy fresh produce, artisanal crafts, and street musicians. If you enjoy the outdoors, there are several great trails and hiking spots in and around Las Cruces. Lastly, Old Mesilla is a beautiful part of town with several shops and award-winning restraunts that is definitely worth checking out!
